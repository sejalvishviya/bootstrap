Chef Sagar S Bajaj

Our corporate chef, Sagar, or as we lovingly call him, "Bajaj" has a knack for the daring dish. With his years of experience working in kitchens overseas and even on cruises, he brings that something different. Sagar's culinary talent can be seen in a very unique menu curated by him and his team of chefs for Dragonfly Experience.

Richard Hargroves

Being a chef, Chef Richard Hargroves has always been an explorer & a lover of flavour & taste. Travelling to the most exotic places, working in different nations and cooking various cuisines has made him gain an edge over others. Global influences, local ingredients and modern techniques is the trinity of his culinary skills.

#ExperienceDragonfly

The biggest venture till date by First Fiddle Restaurants, led by visionary master - Priyank Sukhija, Dragonfly Experience is a high energy restaurant and bar, nestled in Worldmark 1, Aerocity. Climbing through three floors with a bar on each to cater the guests, the episode unfolds upon the spread of Pan Asian fusion and European fusion dishes along with exotic world-class mixology.Dragonfly Experience holds the stand to be an evocative host for series of grand vibes.
